Photography and Me

Over the last couple of years, I have dabbled into photography a lot in my spare time. I would take the occasional pictures of the sky and the flowers. Anything that I personally found beautiful or interesting. But I was never really that serious about it up until last year. Last year I finally made the decision to purchase a camera and a lens and take my interest to a next level. I got sucked in real quick. Everything about photography is just very intense for me. It has been a year since I got my camera and I have added some extra gear. However, photography has somehow become a very relaxing thing for me. I love the idea of preserving a memory or an instance in a frame that we can revisit whenever we want. I really like stopping the world with a click of a button and then re-presenting it back to the world. I will add some of my favorite artists whose work I really enjoy.
